# 11. Transfers
The transfer system in PManager is divided into several parts: regular transfers, free transfers, direct transfers and Bosman transfers.

Bosman Transfers
The Bosman Rule allows players at the end of their contract to sign for other clubs without any compensation fee.

After the 12th round of the league, you can start making offers for players on other teams with a one year contract. However, the sale of the player is not immediate, and must first be decided by an auction. The player will gather all of the offers and accept the one that offers him the highest wage. The auctions for players in this condition will be made on Tuesdays and Fridays at 10:00am (server time). You will only be able to buy 5 players per season through the Bosman Rule. Choose wisely!
The players will sign for their new teams towards the end of the season if the squad of the new team does not exceed 50 players.

All of the players bought through the Bosman Rule will be subject to a 30% selling tax in their first season at their new club.

An important note is that you won't lose your Bosman signings if their teams are reset due to inactivity. They will remain in a "pending" status until the end of season. That is when they will finally move to your team.

Free Transfers
The free transfer market is a market that consists of international and/or former international players that lost their original team due to inactivity and quality players (or big potential) that got lost in inactive teams.

This market works more or less like the Bosman market (read above): the player will choose to sign for the team that offers him the highest wage. The only difference is that, you'll also have to pay a signing fee to the player equal to 12 times the offered wage. This means that, if you offer a wage of 10,000€ then you’ll have to pay a signing fee to the player of 120,000€. There is a 30% selling tax for these players as well. Read further in this section for more information.

The free market auctions are made all Thursdays at 12:00PM (noon) server time. You can find the free transfer market by going to Team >> Transfers >> Transfer List >> Free Transfers.

Regular Transfers
There are several steps you have to follow in order to complete a regular transfer. First, the manager of the selling team must put a player up for sale. After the player is put up for sale, he'll be on the Transfer List (also known as the TL) and a deadline will be set for offers to arrive. This deadline is exactly 3 days after he was first put on the list. During this time, other human managers will have the possibility of making offers (respecting the minimum price set by the player's manager and wage of the player). All bid values will remain hidden in the process: this is called a closed bidding auction. However, if you have a scout, he'll give you an idea of the average top ten (10) bids. The accuracy of this value will depend on your scout's ability.
Once the deadline expires, the player is transferred to the team that offered the selling team the highest bid. Only the 20 next top bidding managers will receive a message telling them that they lost the bid on that player.

Transfer List (for Regular Transfers)
You can access the transfer lists by choosing Team >> Transfers >> Transfer List >> Filter. To simplify the player's search, you can use the filter options that allow you to search in all national transfer lists at the same time.

The players are ordered by their bidding deadline. You have a link to place a bid for any of the players on the Transfer List.. The page is divided into two sections: transfer details and bid details. The transfer details display all of the monetary values related to the purchase. One of the most important aspects you should pay attention to is the wage roof (explained in this section, below). The Bid Details page is where you can place bids on players that you are interested in buying. They are: bid value, player wage and contract duration.

Direct Transfers
You’ll also have the possibility to approach a player even if he’s not on the transfer list. If the current manager accepts the offer then the player will be automatically placed on the transfer list with one bid (the offer you made). Note: other clubs will be able to bid on that player. By default, all players will be approachable.

However, as a manager you’ll be able to change that setting on any player on your team.
The three options that you’ll have are: player available to receive offers; player not available for sale; or reject all offers lower than X.

Every time that you place a player on the transfer market, your club will pay a 1,000€ fee (not applicable on direct transfers). This fee will avoid players being transfer listed with absurd prices, and it will also prevent the market from being flooded with low quality players with absolutely no chance of being sold.

Wage Roof
Basically the wage roof is a set value that defines the maximum sum of wages that you can pay the players on your team. This value influences, not only the wage you can offer players during the transfer process, but it also affects any contract renewals. The sum of all wages cannot surpass the wage roof in normal situations.
In case the wage roof is surpassed (because it was either decreased by the board or by any other situation) a team will only be able to offer a standard 3000€ wage to any player who's about to sign a new contract.
Let's give you an example. Your team has a wage roof of 100,000€ and the wage sum of the team is 90,000€. In this situation you can offer wages (in new contracts) up to 10,000€. Now lets imagine that the wage sum of the team goes up to 101,000€ after you have bought a player. Until you are able to reduce the wage sum below 100,000€, the max wage you'll be able to offer any player will be 3000€.

The wage roof depends basically on how high your team’s reputation is in PM. To learn more about a club's reputation please check the “Teams” section of this manual.

Transfer market taxes
The transfers are not free of taxes. We have two types of taxes in PManager. The first group regards previous clubs of the players.
- First Club of the player: receives 5% of the transfer fee
- Favorite Team of the player : receives 2% of the transfer fee

For more information about these features check the “Players” section of this Manual.

The second one depends on how many games the players have played for the selling team. Here are the values of this fee:
- 0 or 1 game: selling team loses 13% of the transfer fee
- 2 or 3 games: 11%
- 4 or 5 games: 9%
- 6 or 7 games: 8%
- 8 or 9 games: 7%
- 10 or 11 games: 6%
- 12 or 13 games: 5%
- 14 or 15games: 4%
- 16 or 17 games: 3%
- 18 games or more: 2%

As said before there is also a tax for “Bosman Players” and “Free transfer Players”. The tax is the same for both situations: 30% of the transfer fee. Bosman players will remain with this tax for the first season after the transfer. The value will revert to the previous team of the player. Free transfer players will also have this 30% tax until the end of the first season. Players bought one month before the season end will carry the tax to the next season.

Overpriced transfers
All bids should be made in relevance to what could be considered reasonable for the player in question. Overpriced transfers can ruin the good atmosphere between users in the game. Our staff members will have the possibility to adjust and/or cancel transfers if they believe the good spirit of the game is at risk.

The best thing to do if you have received an overpriced transfer is to report the transfer, and not use the money you have received until you receive the staff's verdict. Never try to take advantage of unfair situations; you are likely to be caught and put yourself in delicate situations (i.e. fines, bans, etc).

Transfers between users who share the same PC/Connection
As we said several time, it's allowed to share pc or connection with other users (brother/sister/friends/etc..). Of course these kind of users CANNOT buy/sell players from/to users which are sharing their same connection. This rule is made in order to prevent all suspect about "sharing-users". Not respecting this rule will, unfortunately, lead to account-closure to both users.

Transfers between your Second/Third teams (see cp. 27)
There are some restrictions in this feature though:
- You can’t transfer players between two clubs you control
- You can’t pass players between any of your teams
