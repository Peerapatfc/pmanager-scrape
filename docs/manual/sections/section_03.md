# 3. Registration, activation and team selection
The first step in PManager will be the creation of your account. To create your account, you should follow the “Join” link at the top of the main page. Make sure you fully read our Terms of Service before proceeding to the registration page; you must accept them before registering in the game.

On the registration page make sure you insert full, true and valid data in the required fields. Registrations with incomplete addresses, invalid first names, invalid surnames (last names) or other fake/incomplete details will not be accepted by our staff. Also, you should only register in the country where you currently reside. If you are living in France, but register in Portugal (even if you are of Portuguese nationality) we will either reject your application or move it to the correct country.

After the registration process is complete, you will be put on the validation queue. This means that before you are able to login, our staff must approve your registration. This usually takes one to three days. Once our staff goes through your application, you will then receive an email about the status of your registration (approved or rejected).

In case your application has been rejected, please consider reviewing all of the information that was previously entered on the registration page. The most common causes of a rejected registration are:
- Invalid/fake/incomplete personal details (including address);
- A valid IP address (are you using proxies?);
- Double registration (remember only 1 account is allowed per person).

Once your registration has been accepted, it will be put on hold until a team is assigned to you. You will now be able to log in to the website. Team assignments are made every day around 06:00 am (server time). Once your registration has been accepted, it will take about 24 hours for you to actually receive your team (if there are, in fact, any available teams in your country). You will receive an email once a team has been assigned to you.

If the country you are trying to register in is full (meaning, there are currently no available teams that can be assigned to new accounts), this process will take longer. In this case, please be patient and continue to check your email.

Please be advised that some of the automatic emails we send you at the beginning are likely to end up in the Junk folder of your email account (especially if your provider is Hotmail, AOL, MSN or Yahoo). You should add our domain name to your safe list (@pmanager.org). We never sell your information to anyone! Your application information is used for verification purposes only!
