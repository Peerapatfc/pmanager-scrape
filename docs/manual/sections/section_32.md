# 32. Game Administration
From time to time, the Administration team of PManager appoints regular users as official team members. These user's jobs help us maintain the game, and allow you (a normal user) to have direct contact with someone both close with the administration and with your local community. We have four different staff roles:

Administrator (ADM) are the creators and maintainers of the game. They are responsible for the game's programming and design/development of new features. They are also responsible for fixing any bugs/errors. They are also responsible for the nomination of new staff members. We have splited the "ADM" role we had in the game in two new roles:

Developers (DEV) - The main task of these users is to fix bugs and develop new features.

Community Manager (CM) - These kind of users are in charge of collecting all kind of feedback from the community and bring it to the Developers and those that are in charge of running the game.

Super Moderator (SM) - They are usually responsible for managing all of the issues regarding their local communities (unless they are global SM's who are responsible for all issues regarding every single local community in general). Their main functions are: dealing with cheaters and cheating reports, validating new user accounts and answering emails. They also recommend new ADM's, MOD's and LA's for their local communities.

Moderators (MOD) - Their main function is to moderate our game forums. They are also responsible for the moderation of any other public form of communication inside the game (except private messages which, of course, are not public).

Language Administrator (LA) - Their only job is to keep the language version they are managing up-to-date. They also translate game announcements to their language.

You should know that we fully support our team members. Specifically talking about our SM's, they have all the necessary tools to analyze cheating suspects so we'll backup all their decisions. You should contact us through our support center if you want to make any comments regarding our staff's decisions. Bragging about them on the forums won't really help much as you'll be breaking our rules of behavior.

You should also know that our SM's do not need to explain full details on how they came to the conclusion that someone cheated. If they did that they'd be ruining our cheater-catching system by telling cheaters how to break/go around it. The game is ruled by our rules and if you don't agree with them then it is better for you to not play the game at all. You should also remember that you agree with the rules every time you login into your account, and are bound to comply with them.

All of our staff members (except ADM's) work on a purely voluntary basis. Keep that in mind before judging them unfairly: they're only trying to help us and their local communities by offering their free leisure time.

You can know who these members are by looking at Help >> Administration on the top menu.

The only official way to communicate with the Game Administration is to create a ticket at our support center available at Help >> Contact us (top menu). You will be able to create support tickets that will be answered by our staff, usually in a period of 0 to 7 days. Please avoid creating several tickets with the same content, and be aware that every time you update your ticket by adding a new comment, the ticket will be moved to the bottom of the queue.

PManager also has an internal communication system for blocked users (called the Cheating Support System). All this means is that we do not support email support for any reason whatsoever. Sending an email to any of our available email addresses will not be answered unless we ask you directly to do so.
