# 23. Friendly Matches & Instant Matches
Your team can play a friendly match once a week, if it's not set to play in the national cup (on Mondays or Tuesdays). During off-season, teams can also schedule friendly matches on weekends. Off-season friendly matches have increased attendances. This was made to compensate the losses teams have for not playing official matches (especially important for countries that do not play cup matches during the off-season).
Friendly match gate receipts will be shared between both clubs (50%/ 50%).

Players train after friendly matches. However, only those who have played at least 30 minutes in the game will actually train. For more information about training and friendly matches please read Section 8.

Experience gained in friendly matches is discussed in Section 7.
Read about Friendly Matches and suspensions in Section 14.

PM Fans have a feature called “Friendly Pool”: they will have the opportunity to subscribe to the "Friendly Pool" for a nominal cost which will allow them to be automatically setup with another team a few hours before the friendly match is to be played.

Each Wednesday after 2:00 AM (server-time) the subscriptions will be opened. Once you subscribe, and pay the small cost (5000 €) your team will be added to the weekly friendly pool.
You'll still be able to schedule friendly matches in a normal way (receive/send invitations). However, if you schedule a friendly then your team will be taken out of the pool and your money will be refunded.
Each Sunday, at 2.30 AM (server-time), the draw takes place: all the teams in the friendly pool will be randomly assigned to play a friendly match against another participating team. If the number of participating teams is odd then one of the teams will be left out of the draw - this team's owner will receive a message, have the cost refunded, and still have over a whole day (in most cases) to schedule a friendly match manually.
Instant Friendly Games (IM’s)


Some important aspects of this feature:
- You can only set up a match for the next hour, with 15m breaks. There are limits to the number of matches than can be created on each bracket, depending on the server(s) activity at the time. The max. number of matches per bracket is 15. Once the match is generated, managers will receive a notification at the Match Center and the live match starts immediately.
- PM Supporters can play up to 7 matches per week. Non PM Supporters can play 3 matches per week. You can buy extra matches for 100 PM Coins!
- There are no injuries, suspensions, experience gains, training, fitness losses and/or player/team update of any kind. In fact injured players can play these games.
- There will be ranking that will split all managers between leagues (World class, Professional, Semi-professional, Amateur and Not Ranked).
- You can set the weather for the match. If you want you can also invite a specific team for a match. If you don’t want to invite a team just leave the "Team ID" field at "0".
- You can watch Instant Matches Live.
- The PM Match Viewer is free for all users in these kind of games.

About the ranking, as said, the ranking is divided in 4 leagues:
- World Class league
- Professional league
- Semi-professional league
- Amateur League

Each league may have many series based on the number of managers participating in the Instant Matches. The exception is the World class league that only has 1 series, which means that only the best managers will be on the world class league. Each series will have 22 managers and the ranking is updated every Monday.

During the Monday weekly update of the ranking, the following tasks will be done:
- The top eleven managers of the Professional League will be promoted to the World class league;
- The worst eleven managers of the World class league will be relegated to the Professional league;
- The promotions and relegation of the remaining leagues will be dynamic, depending on the number of ranked managers;

The percentage of managers in each league are:
- World Class: 22 managers
- Professional League: 15% of all ranked managers
- Semi-Professional League: 35% of all ranked managers
- Amateur League: 50% of all ranked managers

Managers get ranking points when they win and draw. They lose points if they lose games. There might be bonuses depending on the fact that you’re playing against a team with higher ranking.

You can find these new pages at the Competitions main menu, at the "Instant Matches" option.
