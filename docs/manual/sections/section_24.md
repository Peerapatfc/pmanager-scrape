# 24. PM Cups
PM Cups can either be created by the administration of the game (official cups) or by normal users. Official cups usually give rewards to the best 4 teams. These rewards may be PM Supporter or PM Coins. From time to time we can offer other prizes as well.

These cups have the following characteristics:
- The cups may have two possible formats: A group qualifying stage with a late knockout stage, or straight knockout stage.
- The date and time of games, as well as the number of games per week, is different for each cup, for example there may be cups with 4 games per week. A cup can have several matches per day but these time slots must be shared between all match days.
- Players do not lose fitness in cup games.
- Players do not gain experience or train in cup games.
- There are no attendances for clubs as well.
- The cup creator has 15 days to manually initiate the draw. Otherwise it will just be initiated automatically.

All users can participate in these cups. The PM Fan requirement drops but it’s replaced by PM Coins. The new cost structure follows below:
- In order to enter a cup, non PM Fans will have to pay 200 PM Coins. PM Fans don’t have to pay anything. Some official cups might require a cost for everyone though.
- All users can create Cups but they have a creation cost though. For Cups with less than 100 teams the cost is 400 PM Coins. You will have to spend 500 PM Coins if you set up a cup for more than 100 teams but less than 200. If you want to create a cup for more than 200 teams, then you will have to spend 600 PM Coins. Cup creators do not have to pay signup fees in their own cup.
