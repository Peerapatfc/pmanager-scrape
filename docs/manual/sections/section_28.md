# 28. Community
Our aim is to have a big, active and interesting community. You can count on us to provide you with tools to improve and expand the community, but it's really up to you to make it happen. You have our word that we'll be actively present on the game forums, reading all of the important stuff you write to us. Be aware that you can't really expect us to answer everything you write as it is almost impossible due to time issues. Although, we would love to do it!

We have internal forums that you can use to communicate with all of the other users. The forums work by subscription mode: for you to have access to a certain forum, you must subscribe to it. The first page after you click on Community >> Forums is the configuration page. You can take a look on your pre-defined forums and subscribe to as many as you wish.

You should, however, remember that we have some rules that you must respect if you want to take an active part in our user community. We'll talk about them in the next section of this manual.
