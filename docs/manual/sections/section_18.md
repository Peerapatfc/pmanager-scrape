# 18. The Manager & Job Center
Managers are basically users. Each and every PManager user has the ability to become a manager once his account has been validated.

I've noticed that I have a wage. How does this work?
This is only a stat based on the previous season performances, and it depends on the team as well. Teams in lower division will pay less than teams in higher divisions. This wage cannot be chosen by the manager and it is automatically set by your team's board.
In case you're a PM Fan you'll have a general stat to see who the best paid managers are in the game. We might be doing something fun with this feature in the future, but for now this is just a stat.

Can I get sacked from my team?
Yes, you can. There are only two ways that you can be sacked:
- Team bankruptcy: read more in Section 15. In case you are sacked due to team bankruptcy, you'll be given the chance to manage another team.
- Inactivity: read more in Section 19.
As you've read, you cannot be fired due to bad results. However, bad results will affect your board's confidence a little bit. Although, they'll be more upset over bankruptcy than they will over results. So, you will never be sacked in case you want to set a long-term strategy for your team, thus sacrificing your short-team strategy as a consequence.

How does the reputation system work?
In the same way as the team's ranking system works, except that manager rankings do not have fixed limits. To find information on how many manager ranking points you'll win (each match), check the “Next Match” page.
At the beginning of each new season, the stats are reset. Career stats are never reset.

What's the Star Award prize?
This is a community prize. Every PM Fan manager has the chance to give 5 monthly votes (not possible to vote on the same manager more than once, monthly) that they can give to other PM Fan managers.
At the end of each month, the top 3 managers will receive a small award which is placed on their profile page for all to see.
The Job Center - Career Management Mode


Career Management Mode is a feature that will change the way you manage clubs in PManager. Up until now you could only manage clubs of your own nationality. With this feature you have the possibility to move to foreign countries. You will find this option going on User (your username) > Job Center.
Here are the full details for this new feature:

- A country can only have up to 25% of their teams controlled by foreign managers.
- All bot teams can receive applications. After an application is made, an auction for the management of the team is open for 7 days. At the end of these 7 days, a decision is made by the board of the club. The decision is purely based on the manager’s reputation. We understand that some of you are not happy with the way manager’s reputation is calculated. You must understand that his formula has a very strong weight of your country’s (ITC) reputation and on your last 4 seasons as a manager (standings are very important). In general we are happy with the way the formula is working but we are always ready to make improvements.
- Once an human manager leaves a team, the team will not get an immediate reset. Instead, the team is kept unchanged for 15 days. This will allow other human managers to apply to this team. If no one applies the team will be reset after the initial 15 days. During these 15 days the team will keep his players, funds and structures.

A new possible manager will inherit these conditions.
- If you move to another team, your team name will only be kept if the team has its original (bot) team name. In case you win the job for a team that recently lost its manager you will have to keep the team’s name. Obviously that you can change it later if you have PM Fan.
- Bot teams finances are reset to 1.000.000€ at the end of each season (excluding teams that are waiting for a reset or teams that are in a middle of a manager auction).
- You can’t apply to your previous team (example: if you are fired due to bankruptcy you cannot apply to your old team).
- Once you win a manager auction for a certain team, you must remain on the team for a complete season. This means that you cannot apply for another team for the next 78 days.
- Once you move to another team all your previous players, funds and structures are kept in your previous team. And your previous team will be available for other managers to pick. We will punish managers that "vandalize" their previous teams just before moving to a new team.
- To apply to another team just open the team’s page and select the option on the top-right of your screen. You can only apply for one club at the same time.
