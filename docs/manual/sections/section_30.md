# 30. Manager's Certificate & Mentorship System
The manager's certificate is a tool that newbies are prompted to use and complete in order to achieve two goals:
- A quick way to learn about the game once they start playing it;
- The community's recognition. Managers without the certificate can't be considered top managers by the rest of the community.

This tool is also very important to the game as it allows you to become eligible to manage a National Team or to become a mentor.

The Certificate is made up of a series of questions about the game as well as some actions you need to complete. It should be quite easy to use!

All those who are able to complete the Certificate will receive a nice surprise at the end!
Mentorship System


With our users help and with this mentorship system we hope to reduce the learning curve new users face themselves with when they start the game.

Here are some infos:
- Mentors are now marked as inactive in case they miss activity regarding their apprentices.
- Mentors are warned whenever they get are set with a new apprentice.
- Apprentices from inactive mentors are now automatically re-assigned to new (active) mentors.
- Mentors can only get 10 apprentices max. per language instead of 40.
- Counting all apprentices in all languages, a mentor can only have 30 max.
- From now on mentors can drop their apprentices out (useful for example if they are not active or you don’t speak the same language).

There are now rewards for mentors in case they are able to integrate their apprentices in the game successfully. Here are the rewards we are giving:
>> Apprentice has been active in the game for 3 months: You get 7 days of free PM Fan.
>> Apprentice has been active in the game for 6 months: You get 8 days of free PM Fan.
>> Apprentice has been active in the game for 12 months: You get 9 days of free PM Fan.
>> Apprentice has been promoted to a higher division: You get 5 days of free PM Fan.
>> Apprentice has bought his own PM Fan: You get 5 days of free PM Fan.
(the length of these bonus might be changed in the future if we find it appropriate)

Regarding these rewards:
- Rewards will only be given in cases the apprentices are registered after 1/09/2009 (1st of September, including). Also, in order to claim a reward, apprentices must have made a login in the last 10 days. They also need to be linked to you as an apprentice for the same period the reward is about. For example, if we’re talking about a 3 months-active award, if the apprentice registration date was 5 months ago and the user is only your apprentice for 2 months you won’t be able to claim the reward until the next month.
- You need to claim rewards in order to get them as they are not assigned automatically. In order to do that you just have to open your apprentice details and click on the "Reward" link.
- You can opt to give your rewards to the apprentice. There’s an option at the Rewards section for that.
- After claiming the reward you will have to wait a couple of days as it will be manually validated by our staff. We have decided to use a manual verification process to prevent abuse. This also means that we can actually reject a reward if we conclude that the mentor did nothing to help the apprentice (we have the necessary tools to analyze the communications made between both users).
