# 21. Season Prizes
At the end of each season there are monetary prizes for the following categories:
- Standings prizes: for 1st, 2nd and 3rd positions
- Top scorer of the series
- Player with the most assists in that series
- Player with the most MOM (Man of the Match) awards in that series
- Player with the best average rating in that series

The prizes depend on the division they are earned, and are as follow (please use the money conversion tool we have at Help >> Currencies to convert these values to your native currency system):

What happens when two players have the same amount of goals/assists/mom's/rating?
In these cases, the prize is awarded to the player with fewer minutes played. In all cases, only one prize will be awarded.
