# 19. Inactivity
From time to time, we delete inactive users from the game. Each week (on Tuesdays at approximately 3:30am server-time), users that have gone more than 1 month (31 days) without logging into the game will be deleted from PManager. This check is performed only once per week, and there's a possibility that you can go over 31 days inactive (e.g. if a manager is inactive for 30 days on a Tuesday he'll only be deleted the Tuesday after, providing he stays inactive), but we can assure you that an inactivity period of 37 days is the absolute maximum. PM Fans will be safe from this inactivity check, though.

When a user is deleted, his team is reset and becomes a bot team. This means that it can become a host for another newbie manager. All players are deleted except those who have international appearances (caps) on their career stats (these players will be put on the free transfer list - read more about this type of transfer market in Section 11), and a new squad is generated.

You'll receive some warnings along the way if you become inactive. However, as it was stated before, if your account is inactive for 31 days, you will be kicked from the game.
