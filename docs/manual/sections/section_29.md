# 29. Behavior
As we told before, if you want to be part of our user community then you really have to respect some rules. These rules apply not only to the forums, but also to every area where you can interact with other users. These other areas are: blogs, internal messages, etc.

What is not allowed:
- Advertising players (except on the ads forum, created for that exact purpose)
- Requesting friendly matches (except on the ads forum).
- Multiple posting of similar comments or threads on the forum (or blog entries), or posting comments not related to the topic (spam).
- Bumping threads on the forum (spam).
- Making any kind of attack on a person, race, nation or religion.
- Starting political discussions.
- Excessive use of swear words and/or insults.
- Excessive use of CAPITAL letters.
- Making cheat accusations (use the proper cheat report form by going to Help >> Report Cheaters).
- Advertising things or services you want to buy/sell in real life.
- Supporting or inciting to the use of illegal software.
- Promoting the use of illegal substances or criminal activities.
- Discussing decisions taken by members of the Administration of the game.
- Creating blog entries, forum threads or posting anything linking to adult/obscene material (porn, etc.).

Exceptions (at forums):
- Only the national team manager can place ads in National Forums about players that belong to the National Team who are currently for sale. If the player belongs to the National Manager's personal team, he is not allowed to do it.
-Sending private messages asking for votes on PIF / National Federations is not allowed and can result in immediate suspension from the election.

Punishments :
- All infractions to these rules may lead to punishments coming from the administration staff. The punishments are decided based on the severity of the infraction. Punishments are also based on the previous history of any infractions by that user. The punishments may vary. They consist of fines, bans from the forums for some time or even the instant termination of your PManager account.
- The forum moderators may move, delete or close topics without notice and/or explanations as they see fit.
- All decisions taken by the administration team and the punishments made cannot be discussed in any public location within the game.
These rules can change without prior notification from the staff.
