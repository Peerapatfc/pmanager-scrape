# 1. Introduction
Planetarium Manager is a free, online football management game. You're a football manager in control of your own team, competing in a fantasy league against other human managers and/or computer-controlled teams (bots).

The game never ends, and never stops. It is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can login to check on your team whenever you want. The game will continue to run even when you log off.

The game is played through your Internet Browser, and you can login from anywhere to manage your team -- provided that you have an Internet connection.

This Manual will help you get started in the game and help you learn all of the details you'll need to know in order to become a successful manager in Planetarium Manager.

Happy reading!

(Note: We will always be talking about EURO when it comes to currency in this manual).
